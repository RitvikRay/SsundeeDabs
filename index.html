A gaming youtuber


Ian Marcus Stapleton, better known by his YouTube username Ssundee, was born on December 2, 1987. He began his YouTube gaming career back in 2010 when he uploaded his first “Minecraft” video. Today, he has over 20 million subscribers on his YouTube channel and uploads videos daily. Throughout his over-a-decade-long career, Stapleton has streamed many different games with many different creators. He even had an alter ego named Derp Ssundee. Recently, Stapleton has been posting “Among Us” gameplay videos. He is a father to two sons and has an incredible internet personality.
